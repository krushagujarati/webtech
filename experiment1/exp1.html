Home

Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU) offers multiple programs ranging from engineering, liberal studies, and management to research and innovation in sustainable energy and technologies. Established in 2007, PDEU continues to empower students with a vision of building a self-reliant and sustainable future for India and the world.

About Us

PDEU is India's first and only energy university that fosters innovation and entrepreneurship in energy education. Recognized as a research-intensive institution, it offers opportunities to explore interdisciplinary studies and promote sustainability.

Research

PDEU is committed to creating a culture of innovation and research. With dedicated research centers like the Centre for Biofuels, the Centre for Energy Studies, and the Centre for Excellence in Geothermal Energy, the university has been driving groundbreaking research in renewable energy, smart grids, and sustainable development.
